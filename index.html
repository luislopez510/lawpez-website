Luis López is a Venezuelan-trained attorney and U.S. legal consultant with a distinguished academic record spanning three institutions across two countries. His formation combines the depth of civil law tradition with a command of the U.S. legal system, cultivated through graduate studies at Loyola University Chicago School of Law.

A prolific legal author and contributor to journals of law and political science, Luis is also the author of Código Salitre, a novel that bridges the literary and legal worlds. Through Lawpez, LLC, he serves individuals and law firms seeking expert support on Venezuelan law, immigration, and cross-border legal matters.
